What Is Sleep Apnea?

Sleep apnea occurs when your breathing repeatedly stops and starts during sleep. It can be caused by a blocked airway (obstructive sleep apnea), brain signal issues (central sleep apnea), or a combination of both.

  • Loud snoring
  • Gasping for air during sleep
  • Daytime fatigue
  • Morning headaches
  • Difficulty concentrating

The Dental Connection

Many people are surprised to learn that dentists can help treat sleep apnea. At our dental clinic, we offer oral appliance therapy‚Äîa custom-made device worn during sleep to keep your airway open.

Oral Appliance Therapy Benefits

  • Travel-friendly (unlike bulky CPAP machines)
  • Non-invasive and silent
  • Clinically proven to reduce snoring and improve sleep quality

Is Oral Appliance Therapy Right for You?

This treatment is ideal for:
