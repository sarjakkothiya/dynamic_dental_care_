Dental Implants

The New All-On-4 Dental Implants Concept

Here at Dynamic Dental Care, we intend to plan all our cases to reduce the total number of visits and treatment duration without compromising oral health and to improve aesthetics and chewing function. With recent advancements in digital dentistry, it is now possible for us to place dental implants after teeth removal and provide temporary fixed teeth in just a week!

What is the All-On-4 dental implant concept?

It is a full-mouth rehabilitation procedure in which four dental implants are placed in one jaw. You can have completely fixed teeth using only four implants if inserted strategically into the jaw bone. Yes, we can replace all your teeth using only four dental implants.

Dynamic Dental Care offers you this very new concept to replace your all missing teeth. It is a quick, durable and painless procedure that gives you great stability and improves your smile and chewing function. It is the best solution for full-arch treatment using tilted implants that provide a fixed full-arch temporary prosthesis within one week. There would be no requirement for bone grafts in this [procedure, so it is the less expensive and most predictable rehabilitation option.

To replace your missing teeth, the dental implant is the best fixed option. But, in the traditional dental implant technique, six to 8 implants are required to replace all teeth in the single jaw.

In the All-on-Four concept, only four implants are strategically placed to replace all teeth of a single jaw. Our specialist can distinguish the main difference between traditional dental implants and the All-on-four concept.

Steps in the process of All-on-four dental implants:

  • Step 1: Examinations, diagnosis, digital x-ray and treatment plan
  • Step 2: Making a surgical template before dental implant surgery
  • Step 3: Extraction of present teeth and strategic placement of dental implant
  • Step 4: Placement of temporary fixed teeth on dental implants.

Total cost of All-on-four implant system

The total cost of this treatment differs from patient to patient. The total cost depends on the number of dental implants, the type and number of teeth restored and whether bone grafting is required. At Aspire Dental Studio, our dental experts examine and do needful 3D x-rays before giving you a full quotation.
